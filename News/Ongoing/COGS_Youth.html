Introduction

In our youth fellowship, we all come from different background and environment. Therefore, this is a good opportunity to gather together to share our thoughts, to get to know more about our Almighty God, to love one another, to build a healthy and strong network together to envision a better church and wider society. In addition, we seek to have more creative and and fun-filled attributes to become a more active youth through various events and a variety of activities provided, such as the meaningful and powerful of God’s messages, entertainments and games, testimonies, musics and songs, exercises, etc. Please feel free to bring in your friends to our fun-filled youth fellowship.
