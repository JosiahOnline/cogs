Our Vision Statement
To provide suitable pre-school activities, for the all-round physical, mental, emotional, social and spiritual growth of children.

Introduction

Good Shepherd Kindergarten emphasizes both the mental and physical development according to Kurikulum Standard Prasekolah Kebangsaan (KSPK 2017). These curriculums are stated with 6 components:

1.The language & communications component
2.The spiritual, attitudes & moral value
3.Humanitarian component
4.The socio – Emotional development component
5.The Aesthetic & Physical component
6.The Science & Technology component


Besides usual curriculum required by Kementerian Pendidikan Malaysia, we also have spiritual teaching, for examples:

1.Worship God and storytelling about god’s word
2.Pray before and after class
3.Pray before having the breakfast
