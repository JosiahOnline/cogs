Welcome to

all family members & friends!

"We are a people of God for the people of Sandakan! We are enjoying the real purpose of life and like to share it with people who are looking for the meaning of life! You can easily find us via Waze or Google map. You can contact us anytime through THE CONTACT section of this website. Do let us know when you will be coming so we can wait for you and serve you! We love to have you with us so come and visit us soon! Every good blessings of God be upon you! See you very soon!"

Your friend,
Canon James Lee
